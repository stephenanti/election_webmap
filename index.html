Interactive webmap - 2020 US Presidential elections

Stephen Anti

New York, June, 2022

This is an interactive webmap of the 2020 US Presidential elections. It shows States and Counties and which of the two candidates, Donald J Trump and Joseph R Biden Jr won and by which percentage. Mapbox GL JS is the main JavaScript library powering the interactive map.Joe Biden won the 2020 presidential election with 306 electoral votes against incumbent Donald Trump who had 232 electoral votes. Biden received 81.2 million of the national popular votes and with Trump getting 74.2 million.
